The Water Crisis Explained

Why Clean Water Matters

Access to clean water transforms lives by improving health, education, and economic opportunities. Yet millions still spend hours each day collecting unsafe water from distant sources.

Waterborne diseases from contaminated water remain one of the leading causes of death in children under five worldwide.

"Water is life, and clean water means health."

- Audrey Hepburn, UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador
